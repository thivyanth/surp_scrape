ENV01

Description 2024 has witnessed some of the intense heat wave episodes in recent history. Extreme heat and intense solar radiation could impact air quality, especially in urban locations. This project will evaluate the possible impacts of heatwave on various air quality parameters in several Indian cities using publically available data, which may be supplemented by dield studies.
