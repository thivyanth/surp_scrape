CT01

Description Increasing renewable energy supply on the grid presents new challenges of matching supply and demand. Demand response can be an effective tool to enable higher renewable integration. No realistic demand models have been built in India yet. In our project feeder level data will be used to understand daily and seasonal demand patterns for different categories of users. We expect to get demand data from an urban utility in a few days. That data needs to be analyzed.
Number of students 1-2
Year of study Students entering their 2nd year, Students entering their 3rd year
CPI 7.5 and above
Prerequisites Must know python and sql
Duration At least 6 month
Learning outcome Data analysis of electricity demand.
Weekly time commitment 1 - 2 times a week. At least 20 hours a week.

CT02

Description Electricity supply in India has a high degree of quality of supply problems. Much can be done to improve the supply infrastructure, however not much has been done in this direction. In this group we are doing research to connect the energy consumption patterns to crops and irrigation requirement. We have logged power consumption data from several transformers and individual farmers. That data can be analyzed to find interesting patterns that contribute to better infrastructure design.
